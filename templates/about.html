About This To-Do List Application

Welcome to your productivity companion! Here’s what you can do with this app:

  • 📝 Add new tasks as soon as they pop into your mind—never forget a thing!
  • ✏️ Edit and update your to-dos as your plans change.
  • ✅ Mark tasks as complete and enjoy the satisfaction of progress.
  • 🗑️ Delete tasks you no longer need—keep your list tidy and focused.
  • ⏰ Set reminders so you’re always on top of your priorities.
  • 📅 Track when tasks were created to see your productivity over time.
  • 🌟 Simple, clean interface designed to help you focus on what matters most.

Whether you’re planning your day or managing a big project, this app is here to help you stay organized and achieve your goals!
